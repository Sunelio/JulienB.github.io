4RoseDeepLane

"Unity project."

About.

In my first year of game programming, I had the opportunity to create a game with a team consisting of 5 game designers and 3 game programmers. The game focused on a gravity-based puzzle concept within a 3D environment, utilizing a 2D camera perspective.

Project Organization and Difficulty

Engine

We were required to use Unity as our game engine, which created some challenges for us, especially in terms of the game physics. Our game relies heavily on a gravity system.

My Task

I was tasked with working on the game's gravity system. The player needed to influence the gravity of their character or objects in the environment. Since Unity doesn't allow complete access to its physics, I had to recreate an entire gravity system .
