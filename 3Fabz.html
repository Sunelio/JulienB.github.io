About.

For the beginning of the third year, we were tasked with creating a game within a week as part of the GameWeek event, with a team consisting of 2 Game Designers, 4 Game Artists, and 2 Game Programmers. Our game aimed to be fun in a somewhat crazy universe: a muscular mouse capable of grabbing objects, along with its friends, a mosquito capable of jumping and making its friends jump, and a fish capable of striking. Each room is timed, and the goal is to solve puzzles to reach the little boy who is polluting.
